# MVP Scope

## Objective
Validate that small home-service businesses will use UpNext as their daily operating system for booking, scheduling, jobs, customers, and payments.

## Success Criteria
- A business can onboard without help and publish/share a booking page.
- A customer can submit a booking request.
- The business can accept it and schedule a job.
- A team member can see and complete the job on mobile.
- The business can track payment status.
- The dashboard gives a useful daily view.

## Included
Auth · org/business setup · services · availability · public booking page · booking inbox · jobs · calendar · customers · team · crew view · checklists · basic photo upload · payment status · Stripe payment link · email notifications · dashboard · settings · **customer portal v1 (magic link, beta v2)**.

## Excluded
Customer self-service portal (password-based / saved cards) beyond v1 · website builder · marketing/SMS campaigns · gift cards · promo codes · auto card holds · auto-charge · payouts · native apps · multi-location · route optimization · advanced reporting.

## Release Standard
Core flow works end to end; auth & permissions enforced; public booking works; jobs schedulable and completable; payment status trackable; email confirmations work; errors handled; key flows tested; analytics + error tracking installed.
