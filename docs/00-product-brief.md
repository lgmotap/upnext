# UpNext Product Brief

## One-Line Description
UpNext is booking, scheduling, customer, team, and payment software for home-service businesses.

## Problem
Home-service businesses juggle jobs across calls, texts, spreadsheets, manual calendars, and disconnected payment tools. The result: missed bookings, scheduling confusion, weak follow-up, and poor visibility into revenue and daily work.

## Target Users
Solo operators and small local teams (2–50) in cleaning, lawn care, carpet cleaning, pressure washing, handyman, painting, pet walking, mobile car wash/detailing, and roofing/inspection services.

## Core Jobs To Be Done
1. Let customers request services online.
2. Help owners approve, schedule, and assign jobs.
3. Help teams know where to go and what to do.
4. Track customers, job history, notes, and payment status.
5. Reduce admin work and missed follow-ups.

## MVP Promise
A home-service business can launch a professional booking page, receive booking requests, manage jobs and customers, assign work to team members, and track payments from one dashboard.

## Differentiation
Faster setup, cleaner UX, strong for solo + small teams, mobile-first for field work, practical before advanced.

## Non-Goals (MVP)
No website builder, native apps, marketing automation, payroll/payouts, or multi-location enterprise workflows.
